Great Tool to Polish Your Idea

Apr 19th, 2013

When you search the Web for resources about entrepreneurship, (lean)startup and so on, you’ll find a lot of articles and advices, but it is very hard to know how to begin, and what’s could be your next move.

I don’t remember how I get on this, but I found an awesome gold nugget : StartitUp

Based on the Lean Startup, it helps me lot with valuable advices, and a lot of articles containing several samples.

It is very simple to use : you go through the step by step guide, answer to some questions about your idea/team/competition etcâ€¦ and it progressively builds your Lean Canvas and helps you to structure your mind.

Also note that these guys are expecting a lot of feedback, and they are super reactive.

So if you have an idea, and you don’t know where to start, go on StartitUp !
