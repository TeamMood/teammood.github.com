MoraleApp Is Shutting Down :-(

October 5, 2016

Today, MoraleApp announced that they are shutting down their service.

That’s a sad news for their users that have to look for an alternative but also for TeamMood who lose a pretty great competitor.

Anyway, TeamMood is working hard to check if it would be possible to migrate from MoraleApp seamlessly, without loosing your historical data.
